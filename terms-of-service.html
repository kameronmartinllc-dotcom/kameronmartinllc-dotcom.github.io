Description of Service

AvA Type 1 is an educational application designed to teach children and families about Type 1 diabetes management through:

  • Interactive educational games
  • Simulated glucose monitoring
  • Practice food logging
  • Carbohydrate counting exercises
  • Optional Bluetooth-enabled stuffed animal companion
IMPORTANT: This app is for EDUCATIONAL PURPOSES ONLY.

Medical Disclaimer

NOT A MEDICAL DEVICE

AvA Type 1 is NOT a medical device and should NEVER be used for:
  • Actual diabetes management or treatment
  • Real blood glucose monitoring
  • Insulin dosing decisions
  • Medical diagnosis
  • Emergency situations
  • Replacing medical equipment or professional care

Educational Simulation Only

All features in the app, including:

  • Glucose readings
  • Insulin calculations
  • Carb counts
  • IOB (Insulin on Board) values
  • COB (Carbs on Board) values

Are SIMULATED FOR EDUCATIONAL PURPOSES ONLY and do not reflect real medical data.

Professional Medical Advice Required

ALWAYS:

  • Consult qualified healthcare professionals for diabetes management
  • Use FDA-approved medical devices for actual glucose monitoring
  • Follow your doctor's treatment plan
  • Seek immediate medical attention for emergencies
  • Verify all medical information with healthcare providers

NEVER:

  • Make treatment decisions based on this app
  • Use simulated values for real insulin dosing
  • Delay seeking medical care
  • Replace prescribed medical devices with this app
