Privacy Policy for AvA Type 1

Last Updated: November 8, 2024

Introduction

AvA Type 1 ("we", "our", or "the App") is a pediatric diabetes education application designed to help children and families learn about Type 1 diabetes management through interactive games and simulations. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Data We DO NOT Collect

AvA Type 1 does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • We do not collect health information or medical data
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All app data is stored locally on your device only and includes:

  • Educational Progress: Game scores, achievements, and learning progress
  • Simulation Settings: User preferences for the diabetes simulation (compression factor, speed)
  • Food Log Entries: Practice food logs used for educational purposes only
  • App Preferences: Theme selection and display settings
  • Bluetooth Connection Status: If using the optional stuffed animal companion feature

This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to our servers (we don't have servers)
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Cannot be accessed by anyone except you

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

AvA Type 1 is designed for children under 13 and their families. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect any personal information from children
  • Parental Control: Parents can delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • No Third-Party Advertising: The app contains no ads or third-party trackers
  • Educational Use Only: All simulations and data are for educational purposes
  • No Online Features: The app does not require an internet connection (except for optional resources)

Bluetooth Permissions

If you use the optional stuffed animal companion feature:

  • Purpose: Connect to a Bluetooth-enabled stuffed animal for glucose alerts
  • Data: Only basic connection status is stored locally
  • Control: You can disable Bluetooth access in iOS Settings at any time
  • No Transmission: Bluetooth data is not transmitted beyond the local device connection

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • We recommend enabling device encryption (enabled by default on iOS)
  • No internet transmission means no interception risk
  • Data is isolated to the app's sandbox

Medical Information Disclaimer

Important: AvA Type 1 is an educational tool only, not a medical device:

  • Simulated glucose readings are NOT real medical data
  • Do not use this app for actual diabetes management decisions
  • Always consult healthcare professionals for medical advice
  • The app does not replace blood glucose monitoring or insulin therapy

Third-Party Services

AvA Type 1 does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services
  • No crash reporting
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud backup services

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no data to:

  • Request access to
  • Request deletion of
  • Request correction of
  • Opt out from

However, you can always:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Disable Bluetooth permissions in iOS Settings
  • Contact us with privacy questions

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing an in-app notification for significant changes

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

International Users

AvA Type 1 is designed for use in the United States and complies with:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) where applicable

If you're in the European Union, we also comply with GDPR's privacy-by-design principles, though we don't collect personal data.

Consent

By using AvA Type 1, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

For Parents/Guardians:

We designed AvA Type 1 with your child's privacy as our top priority. If you have any concerns about privacy or how the app works, please contact us before allowing your child to use the app. You have the right to review any information stored locally on the device by using the app yourself.

Educational Use Statement:

This app is intended solely for diabetes education and awareness. All glucose readings, insulin calculations, and food logs are simulated for learning purposes only. Never use this app as a substitute for professional medical care or actual diabetes management devices.
