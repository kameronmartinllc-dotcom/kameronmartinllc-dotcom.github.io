Fighting for a Type 1 Diabetes Cure

I've lived with type 1 diabetes since 1992. My six-year-old daughter was diagnosed on September 27, 2022. I'm uninsured and manage without a CGM or pump, while she uses modern tools. Growing up when T1D felt scarier shaped me. The contrast between our experiences drives this site: explain the condition clearly, build affordable tools families can use now, and apply AI to keep attention and resources focused on real progress toward a cure.
